"Home for Everyone"

Train in Boxing, Boxing Fitness, Kickboxing, Strength & Conditioning for all ages.
We staff at least one trainer per four members at a time to offer personal, hands on approach to learning proper technique. Coaches work with each member to help realize their goals, whether it be for fitness, competition, or self-defense.

  • Youth Programs
    Programs geared for kids 6-12 help young people learn fundamentals, improve fitness in a safe environment.
  • A Variety of Fighting Styles
    Training Includes: Professional * Amatuer * Recreational Boxing & Youth Boxing
  • Rates
    Rates as low as $50/month. 1 on 1 coaching available.

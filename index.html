Our Mission

At B&F Boxing Gym, we are a “Home for Everyone”. All levels are welcome from any background. Our mission is to empower our community through a boxing program that encourages mentorship, discipline, respect, health, and hard work. We aim to make a positive impact on the upcoming generation by offering a welcoming youth program for kids.


  • Youth Programs
    Programs geared for kids 6-12 help young people learn fundamentals, improve fitness in a safe environment.
  • A Variety of Fighting Styles
    Training Includes: Professional * Amatuer * Recreational Boxing & Youth Boxing
  • Veteran Owned
    Offering discounts for veterans and families.

Youth Boxing

Learn the fundamentals of boxing, develop self-defense skills and improve self-confidence, coordination and strength.


Youth classes teach basic boxing skills and techniques. Class includes cardio, shadow boxing, bag work and basic self-defense. Participants will work with the bags and learn stance, guard, movement, the jab, cross, hook combination and more.
